About Viber


Viber is a calling and messaging app that connects people–no matter who they are, or where they’re from. Each month, hundreds of millions of people connect, for free, with their loved ones via messaging, high-quality voice and video calls and more. All Viber calls and chats are protected by built-in end-to-end encryption, so you can be sure that your conversations are always secure.

Frequently Asked Questions


What is Viber and how does it work?

Viber is a free to download app that allows users to make free calls, send texts, pictures and video messages to other Viber users. It works on both mobile and computer and can be used to connect with people around the world. As of February 2015, the messaging app claims 236 million active monthly users.

Is Viber safe?

Yes! Viber uses end-to-end encryption. It protects messages from being read by a third party. The message coming from your device is encrypted before it is sent to your contact.

Does Viber use a lot of data?

Viber, another highly popular messaging app, offers the following for the usage of data: The use of data during a Viber call equals to 240 KB per minute up and down, 14 MB per hour up and down. Text messages are under 1Kb (125 B) per 500 characters.
